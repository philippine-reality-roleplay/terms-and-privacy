## Privacy Policy
 
### 1. Data We Collect
- **Discord ID** — used for account linking, whitelist, and moderation.
- **IP Address** — used for anticheat, ban enforcement, and abuse prevention.


We do **not** collect email addresses, payment card details, or other personal info directly — donation payments are processed by third-party payment providers (e.g. PayPal, GCash) and are subject to their own privacy policies.
 
### 2. How We Use Data
- Verify identity and prevent duplicate/banned accounts.
- Detect and prevent cheating, abuse, and ban evasion.
- Enforce server rules and investigate incidents.
### 3. Data Sharing
We do not sell or share your Discord ID or IP with third parties, except:
- When required by Philippine law or legal process.
- To prevent fraud, cheating, or harm to the Server/community.
### 4. Data Retention
Discord ID and IP logs retained as long as needed for moderation/anticheat purposes, or until account/ban data is manually purged by staff.
 
### 5. Your Rights
You may request info on data held about your account, or request account data deletion, via Discord staff contact — subject to legitimate moderation/security needs (e.g. ban records may be retained to prevent evasion).
 
### 6. Security
Reasonable technical measures (e.g. encrypted backups, access-restricted database) used to protect stored data. No system is 100% secure.
 
### 7. Children's Privacy
No age-verification system currently in place. If you believe a minor's data was collected inappropriately, contact staff for review.
 
### 8. Changes to This Policy
Policy may be updated; changes announced via Discord.
 
### 9. Contact
Privacy questions: official Discord server, staff/admin team.
